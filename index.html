Thiruvananthapuram Trip Itinerary

Dates: November 24 - November 28, 2024

Estimated Cost: 9,000 INR per person

Day 1: November 24 (Sunday)

7:00 PM

Board Kerala State Transport AC Sleeper bus from Bengaluru (1,406 INR per person). Overnight travel to Thiruvananthapuram.

Day 2: November 25 (Monday)

9:00 AM

Arrive in Thiruvananthapuram, check in to the BNB, freshen up.

11:00 AM

Explore the city by foot or public transport.

Suggested Places to Explore:

  • Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple
  • Napier Museum and Art Gallery
  • Kuthira Malika Palace
  • Kovalam Beach (if time permits)

Evening

Relax at the BNB or explore the local food scene.

Day 3: November 26 (Tuesday)

6:00 AM

Pick up rented vehicles.

7:00 AM

Depart for Ponmudi Hills. Spend the day enjoying the scenic views and small hikes.

5:00 PM

Return to Thiruvananthapuram. Relax or explore the city in the evening.

Day 4: November 27 (Wednesday)

9:00 AM

Check out from the BNB. Free time for last-minute sightseeing or relaxing at Kovalam Beach.

Evening

Board the bus back to Bengaluru.

Day 5: November 28 (Thursday)

Morning

Arrive back in Bengaluru.
